Choose Pakistan as Your Next Outsourcing Destination

Pakistan is rapidly becoming a leading outsourcing destination, boasting a vibrant community of over 700 outsourcing firms employing over 1.2 million individuals. This sector contributes significantly to the economy, generating upwards of $22 billion annually, with an impressive growth rate exceeding 12 percent each year. Its strategic positioning in Asia, along with a skilled workforce, cost-effective labor, and robust infrastructure, gives Pakistan a competitive advantage over other outsourcing hubs.

A decade ago, numerous outsourcing enterprises established operations in Pakistan, enticed by the prospect of accessing a workforce at competitive wages. Today, Pakistan stands as a global frontrunner in business process outsourcing (BPO), with annual revenues amounting to billions of US Dollars. Given these favorable conditions, there's ample rationale for businesses to explore outsourcing opportunities within Pakistan.
